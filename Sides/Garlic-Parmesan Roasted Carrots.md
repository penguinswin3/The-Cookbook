

 #Vegetarian #Easy #Healthy 

**4 Servings

---

- 2lbs small to medium carrots, peeled (about 15)
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 3/4c freshly grated parmesan, divided
- kosher salt
- freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley

---

1) Preheat oven to 425Â°. Cut carrots crosswise on a bias into 2" pieces. Cut thicker pieces in half lengthwise. 
2) In a bowl, toss carrots with olive oil, 1/2 cup parmesan, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and ground black pepper. Spread on a foil lined baking sheet in a single layer. 
3) Bake carrots until caramelized in some spots, about 25-30 minutes.
4) Transfer carrots to serving bowl. Toss with lemon juice, parsley, and the remaining 1/4 cup of parmesan.
