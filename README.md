# Guacamole

Some people call it guac.

#dip #vegan

**4 Servings, 200g**

---

- 1 avocado
- *.5 teaspoon* salt
- *1 1/2 pinches* red pepper flakes
- lemon juice

---

Remove flesh from avocado and roughly mash with fork. Season to taste
with salt, pepper and lemon juice.
