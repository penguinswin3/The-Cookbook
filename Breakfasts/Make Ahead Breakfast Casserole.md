The perfect meal for someone (Anthony) to put in the oven before the rest of the group wakes up.

#MeatlessOption #MakeAhead 

9x13 Tray

---
- 20 oz shredded hash browns
- 1 lb crumbled sausage ( #MeatlessOption use imitation breakfast sausage)
- 1 lb bacon ( #MeatlessOption use another pound breakfast sausage)
- 1 small onion diced
- 1/2 red bell pepper diced
- 1/2 green bell pepper diced
- 8 eggs
- 1 12oz can evaporated milk
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 2 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
- 2 Green onion angle cut
- salt to taste
- pepper to taste
---

1) Thaw shredded hash browns and drain excess water
2) Sear meats. Cook through 50% of the way.
3) Combine the onion, peppers, eggs, evaporated milk, and Italian seasoning in a bowl and whish until smooth.
4) Set aside 1/2 cup of the cheese for the topping. Place your veggies and cheese in a greased 9×13 casserole dish. Pour the egg mixture over the veggies and top with the reserved cheese.
5) In the morning, Preheat oven to 350F
6)  Bake for about 55 to 65 minutes. Serve immediately.
